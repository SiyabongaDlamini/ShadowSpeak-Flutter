# ShadowSpeak - Master Your Voice

![ShadowSpeak Banner](https://img.shields.io/badge/ShadowSpeak-Premium-indigo)
![Build Status](https://github.com/SiyabongaDlamini/ShadowSpeak-Flutter/actions/workflows/build.yml/badge.svg)

ShadowSpeak is a premium Flutter application designed to help you master your presentation skills by shadowing the world's best speakers.

## ‚ú® Key Features
- **Live Camera Shadowing**: Real-time camera preview while watching expert videos.
- **Voice Recording**: Actual audio recording of your practice sessions for self-review.
- **Premium UI**: Vibrant Material 3 design with smooth transitions and glassmorphic elements.
- **Progress Tracking**: Weekly goals and practice history.

> 2. Open `ios/Runner.xcworkspace` in Xcode.
> 3. Connect your iPhone and click **Run**.
